Privacy Policy

Chain Reaction Game by Kaiross

Introduction

Welcome to Chain Reaction ("we", "our", or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you play our game.

Data We Collect

Chain Reaction is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Essential Cookies: We use localStorage to save your game preferences (grid size, player count, GDPR consent) locally on your device. This data never leaves your browser.
  • Online Play Data: When you play online, we temporarily store your game session data (room codes, game state) on our servers. This data is automatically deleted when the game ends.
  • No Personal Information: We do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

How We Use Your Data

  • To remember your game preferences
  • To enable online multiplayer functionality
  • To improve game performance and user experience

Cookies and Local Storage

We use essential cookies only:

  • GDPR Consent: Remembers if you've accepted our cookie notice
  • Game Settings: Stores your preferred grid size and player count

We do NOT use:

  • Tracking cookies
  • Advertising cookies
  • Third-party analytics
  • Social media trackers

Third-Party Services

Chain Reaction uses:

  • Google Fonts: For typography. Google may collect anonymous usage data. Google Privacy Policy
  • Socket.io: For real-time multiplayer communication (no personal data collected)

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • No storage of personal information on our servers
  • Automatic deletion of game session data after games end

Your Rights (GDPR)

Under GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access your data (though we store none personally identifiable)
  • Delete your data (clear your browser's localStorage)
  • Withdraw consent (stop using the service)
  • Data portability (not applicable as we don't store personal data)

Children's Privacy

Chain Reaction is a family-friendly game suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children or adults.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.
